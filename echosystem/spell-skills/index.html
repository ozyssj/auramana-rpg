    FEAT SKILLS

    Elemental Affinity I

    Learn two 1st-tier spells from one of your elemental affinities.

    Fireball I

    Prerequisites: Flame Spell III

    You cast a sphere of fire at a designated location within a 30 m radius (100 feet). The sphere has a 3m (10 foot) radius and deals 1d8 fire damage for every 5 MP spent.

    Higher Leves: Each level of the spell increases, its range increase by 3m (10 feet), radius increases by +1,5m (5 feet) and its cost decreases by -1 MP per die of damage. Maximum level: V.

    Mage Armor I

    Prerequisites: None

    You ward yourself with magical energy, gaining 1 point of damage reduction for every 5 MP spent on the spell (does not stack with armor DR). The effect lasts for 10 minutes but can be extended with a free action.

    Higher Leves: Each level of the spell increases, its cost decreases by -1 MP per point of damage reduction. Maximum level: V.
